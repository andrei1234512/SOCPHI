🚨 Phishing Indicators
⚠️
Urgent Requests

Messages demanding immediate action such as "verify now", "account will be locked", or "respond within 24 hours".

🌐
Fake Domain Names

Attackers mimic trusted websites using look-alike domains like g00gle.com, paypaI.com, or faceb00k.net.

👤
Generic Greetings

Phishing emails avoid using your real name and use greetings such as "Dear User", "Dear Customer", or "Account Holder".

📎
Unexpected Attachments

Attachments claiming invoices, delivery receipts, or bank statements often contain malware.

Protection Tips
// Best practices for Filipino netizens
🔐
Password Manager
Use Bitwarden or 1Password to generate and store unique strong passwords for every account.
📲
Enable 2FA
Even if your password is stolen, 2FA stops attackers. Use Google Authenticator over SMS.
🕵️
Use a VPN
A VPN encrypts your traffic on public Wi-Fi, preventing hackers from intercepting your data.
🧹
Audit Social Media
Set accounts to private. Remove birthdays and phone numbers. Oversharing helps identity thieves.
📧
Spot Phishing
Hover over links before clicking. Legitimate companies never ask for passwords via email.
💳
Monitor Accounts
Review bank statements regularly. Set up alerts. Report suspicious transactions to BSP or your bank.
⚖️
Know PH Laws
RA 10173 (Data Privacy Act) and RA 10175 (Cybercrime Act) protect Filipinos from identity theft.
🔄
Update Software
Enable auto-updates on your OS and apps. Security patches fix known vulnerabilities.
🗑️
Delete Old Accounts
Unused accounts store your data and are breach risks. Close accounts you no longer use.

📚 Why Encryption Matters
🛡️
What is Encryption?

Converts readable data into an unreadable format that can only be decoded with the correct key.

🔑
End-to-End Encryption

Used by Signal. Only sender and receiver can read messages — not even the company can access them.

🏦
HTTPS

The 🔒 in your browser means TLS/SSL encryption. Never enter sensitive data on HTTP sites.

📂
Full Disk Encryption

Enable BitLocker (Windows) or FileVault (Mac) to protect data if your laptop is stolen.
